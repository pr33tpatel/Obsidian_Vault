## Big O Notation
- defines an algorithms time complexity and efficiency
#### Types of Big O's
1. $O(1)$ - constant *(Best)*
2. $O(log\space n)$ - logarithmic  
3. $O(n)$ - linear
4. $O(n^2)$ - quadratic (polynomial)
5. $O(2^n)$ - exponential *(Worst)*
